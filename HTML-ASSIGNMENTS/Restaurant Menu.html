United Food palace

About the Restaurant

United Food palace is a popular multi-cuisine family restaurant in Benz Circle, Vijayawada, specializing in authentic Andhra, Biryani, North Indian, and Chinese dishes. Known for its cozy, family-friendly ambience, it offers dine-in, takeaway, and delivery services daily from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM.
